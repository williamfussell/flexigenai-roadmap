FAQ

How often are new features released?
Major releases occur every 6 months with significant platform capabilities. Minor releases provide quarterly feature enhancements and improvements.
When is FlexiGenAI 2.0 launching?
FlexiGenAI 2.0 is scheduled for July–November 2025, introducing the no-code agentic era with complete UI/UX revamp and AI-guided development.
